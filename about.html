ABOUT MINTY HEATING & PLUMBING

We provide better and smarter ways to serve our you.

We are proud to be recognised as one of the leading plumbing and heating specialists in the TW and SL postcode areas, delivering dependable emergency, residential, and commercial plumbing services. As a registered plumber and fully qualified Gas Safe engineer, we combine modern techniques with traditional craftsmanship to provide safe, efficient, and high-quality solutions for every property.

Our company is built around professionalism, reliability, and customer care. Whether you are facing an unexpected plumbing emergency, planning a new installation, or maintaining an existing system, we are committed to offering expert advice, fast response times, and exceptional workmanship on every job. We invest in the latest tools and technology to stay ahead in the industry and ensure our customers receive long-lasting, energy-efficient results.

Modern Plumbing Solutions You Can Trust

We continually explore better and smarter ways to serve our customers. From advanced diagnostic tools to efficient installation methods, our approach ensures minimal disruption, maximum performance, and complete peace of mind. We specialise in trenchless sewer replacement, allowing us to repair underground pipework quickly and effectively without large-scale excavation. This method is ideal for both homeowners and commercial properties.
