Landlord Gas Safety Checks FAQ

1. Do you provide landlord gas safety checks near me?
Yes, we provide local landlord gas safety checks and gas safety check near me services across the UK.
2. Do you carry out British Gas gas safety checks?
Yes, all inspections follow professional British Gas gas safety check standards for safety and reliability.
3. Are your engineers Gas Safe registered?
Yes, all inspections include a full Gas Safe Register check completed by certified engineers.
4. How often is a gas safety inspection required?
Landlords must carry out gas safety checks once every 12 months to remain legally compliant.
5. How long does a landlord gas safety check take?
A standard gas safety inspection usually takes between 30 and 60 minutes, depending on the number of gas appliances in the property.
6. Will tenants receive a copy of the gas safety certificate?
Yes, landlords must provide tenants with a copy of the gas safety certificate within 28 days of the inspection or before a new tenancy begins.
